Keep your cargo journey on track with Intercontinental Rail

Transporting cargo across countries is challenging, and even more so when it involves crossing entire continents. Varying weather conditions, geopolitical instabilities and limited access to landlocked regions can all come in the way of seamless transit.

Intercontinental Rail helps your business overcome these challenges with speed, reliability and efficiency. We offer containerized transport on our rail network, covering multiple countries across Europe and Asia. While rail is our primary mode of transport, we also integrate with Barge, Feeder and trucks to overcome geographical limitations, ensuring an unbroken supply chain covering the Middle Corridor.

Partner with us for seamless Asia-Europe transportation with easy access to Turkey, Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Georgia and Central Asia.

Between the continents. Across the region

Our Intercontinental Rail provides unparalleled coverage across several markets, connecting Asia with Europe as well as facilitating transport within each continent.

  • Asia to and from Europe, including Southeast and Northeast Asia to Europe
  • China to Europe via Germany, France, Benelux, Poland, Denmark, Sweden, Spain, UK and the Czech Republic
  • Short sea-rail from Taiwan/ASEAN countries to Europe via China
  • Central Asia to and from China and Europe
